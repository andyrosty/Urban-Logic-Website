Welcome to Urban Logic Limited

Urban logic limited was formed as a response to the economic need and the opportunity to use the qualifications, extensive industry experience, knowledge and skills of a team of young, vibrant and highly ambitious entrepreneurs led by Mr. Ampadu Acheampong to empower themselves through self employment. Urban logic has been operational since September 2009. We provide our clients with the best service, in an honest and timely manner and conduct our business with integrity. We hereby introduce to you Urban Logic Limited and hope to do business with you in the future.

Sector of Operation

The business operates in two complimentary sectors namely;Infrastructural support and installation of heterogeneous LAN/WAN environments for large and small companies.Design, Construct and Supervision of electrical installations and maintenance.
